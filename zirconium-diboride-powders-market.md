<p><p>Zirconium diboride powders are categorized into two types: nano grade and micro grade. Nano grade powders have particle sizes in the nanometer range, typically below 100 nanometers. These powders offer unique properties such as high surface area and enhanced reactivity due to their small size. On the other hand, micro grade powders have larger particle sizes, typically ranging from 1 to 10 micrometers. They are commonly used in applications where larger particle sizes are required, such as in refractory materials or as a protective coating. Both nano and micro grade powders have their specific uses and benefits depending on the intended application.</p></p>
