<p><p>There are two main types of Warranty Management Systems (WMS) market: On Premise and Cloud. On Premise refers to a system that is installed and maintained on a company's own servers and infrastructure. It provides complete control and customization but requires a higher initial investment and regular maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-based WMS is hosted on a third-party server and accessed via the internet. It offers greater scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness as users can access it from anywhere with an internet connection, without the need for hardware or software installation.</p></p>
