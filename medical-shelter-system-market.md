<p><p>The Medical Shelter System market comprises two main types: Soft Medical Shelter System and Hard Medical Shelter System. Soft Medical Shelters are lightweight and easy to deploy, offering temporary medical facilities during emergencies or disasters. They are typically made of fabric or inflatable materials and provide mobility and flexibility. On the other hand, Hard Medical Shelters are more robust and permanent structures. They are constructed using materials like aluminum or steel and deliver long-term medical facilities in areas with ongoing healthcare needs or military operations. Both systems serve to provide medical support, but differ in their durability and duration of use.</p></p>
