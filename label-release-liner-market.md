<p><strong>The Label Release Liner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paper Based</li><li>Film Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Label release liner is a backing material used in self-adhesive labels. Paper-based release liners are made from paper, offering good flexibility and cost-effectiveness. They are commonly used for applications requiring low tack adhesion. Film-based release liners, on the other hand, are made from synthetic films like polyester or polyethylene. These liners are highly durable, resistant to moisture and temperature, and are commonly used in applications requiring high tack adhesion. Both paper-based and film-based release liners cater to different adhesive requirements in various industries.</p></p>
