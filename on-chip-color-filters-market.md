<p><strong>The On-Chip Color Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bayer Filter</li><li>RGBE Filter</li><li>RYYB Filter</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The on-chip color filters market includes various types of filters used in image sensors to capture colors. The Bayer filter is the most widely used, employing a pattern of red, green, and blue filters in a 2x2 grid. RGBE filter, on the other hand, adds an additional filter for enhanced image quality and low-light performance. RYYB filter combines two green filters to improve low-light sensitivity. Lastly, there are other types of filters in the market that offer different filter patterns or utilize different colors to enhance color reproduction and image quality in image sensors.</p></p>
