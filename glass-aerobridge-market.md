<p><p>Glass Aerobridge is a type of bridge used at airports for boarding and deplaning passengers. It comes in two market types: Electromechanical Elevation System and Hydraulic Elevation System. The Electromechanical Elevation System uses an electric motor and mechanical components to raise and lower the bridge. On the other hand, the Hydraulic Elevation System employs hydraulic power to control the movement of the aerobridge. Both systems serve the same purpose of providing a safe and convenient passage for travelers between the terminal and the aircraft.</p></p>
