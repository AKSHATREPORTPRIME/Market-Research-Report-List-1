<p><p>The military surveillance optronics market can be classified into two types: multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral optronics utilize sensors that capture information from different wavelength bands, providing images with enhanced detection and identification capabilities. Hyperspectral optronics, on the other hand, gather data from a wide range of contiguous spectral bands, allowing for detailed analysis and identification of various objects and materials. Both types of optronics enable military surveillance systems to gather valuable information, enhancing situational awareness and aiding in decision-making processes.</p></p>
