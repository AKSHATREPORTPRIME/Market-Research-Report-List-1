<p><p>The milled ceramic ball market is categorized into two types: inert ceramic balls and active ceramic balls. Inert ceramic balls have high strength and excellent chemical stability, making them suitable for various applications such as catalyst supports and thermal storage. On the other hand, active ceramic balls have added functionality, such as the ability to release negative ions or far infrared radiation, making them ideal for applications in healthcare, water filtration, and environmental protection. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different industries and needs within the market.</p></p>
