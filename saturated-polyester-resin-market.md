<p><p>The saturated polyester resin market includes two main types: hybrid saturated polyester resins and pure saturated polyester resins. Hybrid resins are a combination of saturated and unsaturated polyester resins, resulting in enhanced properties such as improved flexibility and toughness. These resins are commonly used in various applications such as automotive coatings and electrical insulation. On the other hand, pure saturated polyester resins are made solely from saturated polyester, offering excellent chemical resistance and durability. These resins find applications in industries like construction, appliances, and packaging.</p></p>
