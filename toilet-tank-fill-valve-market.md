<p><p>The toilet tank fill valve market consists of two main types: float ball and float cup. Float ball fill valves use a ball-shaped float that rises and falls with the water level in the tank, controlling the valve's opening and closing. Float cup fill valves, on the other hand, have a cup-shaped float that performs the same function. Both types help in regulating the water level by stopping the flow when the tank is full. These valves play a crucial role in maintaining the proper functioning of toilet tanks.</p></p>
