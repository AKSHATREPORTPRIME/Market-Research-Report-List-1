<p><p>Nickel market types encompass the extraction and processing of nickel ore. Nickeliferous Limonite Smelting involves the smelting or melting of limonite, a type of nickel ore with high iron content. This method allows for the separation of nickel from iron. Meanwhile, Garnierite Smelting involves the smelting of garnierite, a specific type of nickel ore rich in nickel and magnesium. This process is further refined to extract and purify the nickel content from garnierite ore. These market types are crucial in supplying nickel for various industrial applications.</p></p>
