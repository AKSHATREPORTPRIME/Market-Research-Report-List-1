<p><strong>The Camshaft Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) Bearings</li><li>Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Camshaft bearings are categorized based on the type of engine they are designed for. Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) bearings are used in engines with one camshaft located in the cylinder head, while Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) bearings are used in engines with two camshafts located in the cylinder head. SOHC engines are simpler and more cost-effective, while DOHC engines typically offer better performance and fuel efficiency. Both types of bearings play a crucial role in supporting the camshafts to ensure smooth engine operation.</p></p>
