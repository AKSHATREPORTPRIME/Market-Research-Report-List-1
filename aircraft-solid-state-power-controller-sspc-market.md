<p><p>The Aircraft Solid State Power Controller (SSPC) market includes two main types: Single-Phase Controller and Three-Phase Controller. Single-Phase Controllers are used in aircraft electrical systems to regulate and control electrical power on a single phase. They are designed to handle lower power loads and are commonly used in smaller aircraft. On the other hand, Three-Phase Controllers are utilized in larger aircraft, as they are capable of managing higher power loads and offer enhanced efficiency in controlling electrical power across three phases.</p></p>
