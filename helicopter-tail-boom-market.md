<p><p>The helicopter tail boom market consists of different types based on their design and flexibility. Rigid helicopter tail boom refers to a fixed structure that provides no flexibility during flight. Semi-rigid helicopter tail boom offers some degree of flexibility through the use of dampers or elastomeric bearings. Fully articulated helicopter tail boom provides the highest level of flexibility, allowing the tail rotor to move independently from the main rotor. Other types may include hybrid designs or unconventional tail boom configurations. These variations cater to different helicopter models and their specific operational requirements.</p></p>
