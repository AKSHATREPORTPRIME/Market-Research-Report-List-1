<p><p>The Infrared Fiber Laser market can be categorized into two types: Continuous Type and Impulse Type. Continuous Type lasers emit a continuous stream of infrared light, making them suitable for applications such as material processing and manufacturing. On the other hand, Impulse Type lasers emit short pulses of infrared light, enabling precise and controlled energy delivery. This type finds application in areas where high precision and accuracy are required, such as scientific research and medical procedures. Both types cater to different needs within the infrared fiber laser market.</p></p>
