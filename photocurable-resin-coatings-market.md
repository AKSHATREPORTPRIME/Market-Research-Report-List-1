<p><p>Photocurable resin coatings are widely used in various industries for their quick-curing properties under UV light exposure. This market can be categorized into three main types: monomers, oligomers, and photoinitiators. Monomers are the basic building blocks that polymerize and form the resin coating. Oligomers are larger molecules that provide strength and durability to the coating. Photoinitiators are compounds that initiate and speed up the curing process when exposed to UV light. These three types work together to create a high-quality, efficient, and durable photocurable resin coating.</p></p>
