<p><ul><li>Electroplated Wire</li><li>Resin Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diamond saw wires market consists of two main types: electroplated wire and resin wire. Electroplated wire is coated with a layer of diamond particles through electroplating, providing high cutting efficiency and precision. It is commonly used in applications that require fast cutting speeds. On the other hand, resin wire is made of diamond particles embedded in a resin matrix, providing flexible cutting characteristics and better longevity. This type of wire is suitable for cutting fragile materials or when a smooth finish is desired.</p></p>
