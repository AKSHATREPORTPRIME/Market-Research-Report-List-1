<p><p>Multiple orifice valves are used in various industries for controlling the flow of fluids or gases. There are two main types of multiple orifice valves: manual and automatic. </p><p>Manual multiple orifice valves require human intervention for adjustment or operation. They are commonly used in applications where the flow rate needs to be controlled manually or where frequent changes in flow rate are not required.</p><p>On the other hand, automatic multiple orifice valves are designed to operate and adjust the flow rate automatically without human intervention. These valves are often used in applications where precise and continuous flow control is necessary or where remote operation is required.</p><p>Both manual and automatic multiple orifice valves play important roles in industrial processes, each offering distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
