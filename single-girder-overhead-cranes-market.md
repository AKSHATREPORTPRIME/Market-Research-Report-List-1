<p><p>A single-girder overhead crane, also known as a single-beam bridge crane, is a type of lifting equipment commonly used in industrial settings. It consists of a single beam that spans across the workspace, supported by end trucks on either side. The hoist, which carries the load, is mounted on the bridge girder and moves along the length of the beam.</p><p>The single-girder overhead crane market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for efficient and cost-effective lifting solutions in various industries, such as manufacturing, construction, and logistics, is driving the market growth. These cranes are known for their high load capacity, flexibility, and ease of operation, making them ideal for handling heavy loads in limited spaces.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology, such as the integration of automation and Internet of Things (IoT) capabilities, are further fueling the market growth.
