<p><p>Fluorescence immunoassay analyzers are used for detecting and quantifying specific molecules in biological samples. They include various types such as enzyme-linked fluorescent immunoassay (ELFI), chemiluminescence immunoassay (CLIA), radioimmunoassay (RIA), and other emerging markets. ELFI utilizes enzymes to produce fluorescence signals, while CLIA relies on chemical reactions to produce light signals. RIA utilizes radioactive substances to detect the target molecules. Other emerging markets refer to the development of new technologies within the fluorescence immunoassay field. These analyzers play a vital role in medical diagnostics and research by providing accurate and sensitive detection of substances in samples.</p></p>
