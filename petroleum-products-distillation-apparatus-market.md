<p><p>The petroleum products distillation apparatus market consists of two types: Type I and Type II. They are commonly used in industries such as fuel production and petrochemicals. On the other hand, Type II apparatus are built for high distillation temperatures and are used for processing heavier petroleum products like asphalt.
