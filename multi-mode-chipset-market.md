<p><p>The multi-mode chipset market consists of two main types: integrated chipset and non-integrated chipset. On the other hand, non-integrated chipsets comprise separate chips for different functions, allowing for more flexibility in customization and higher performance. These chipsets are often utilized in applications that require specific functionalities and high processing power, such as gaming consoles and high-end networking equipment.</p></p>
