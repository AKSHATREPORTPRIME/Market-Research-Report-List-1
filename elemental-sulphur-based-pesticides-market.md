<p><p>Elemental Sulphur Based Pesticides are available in two main types: dispersion and powder. In the dispersion market, these pesticides are formulated as a liquid with elemental sulphur particles suspended in water or oil. This allows for easier application onto crops using spray equipment. On the other hand, the powder market offers elemental sulphur in a dry powder form. This is often used for dusting or as a seed treatment method. Both dispersion and powder forms provide effective pest control for various agricultural applications.</p></p>
