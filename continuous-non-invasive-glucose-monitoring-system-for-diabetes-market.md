<p><p>Continuous non-invasive glucose monitoring systems (CNGMS) for diabetes are devices used to monitor blood glucose levels without the need for regular fingersticks. There are two types of CNGMS in the market - wearable and implantable. Wearable CNGMS consists of sensors that are attached externally to the body, typically on the skin, and continuously measure glucose levels. Implantable CNGMS, on the other hand, involves sensors that are placed inside the body, usually under the skin or within a blood vessel, providing continuous glucose monitoring. Both types offer the advantage of convenience and continuous monitoring for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
