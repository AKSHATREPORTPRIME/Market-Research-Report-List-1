<p><p>The automotive steel tube market can be categorized into two types: welded steel tube and seamless steel tube. Welded steel tubes are manufactured by joining steel sheets together through welding processes. They are commonly used in applications where a high level of structural strength is required. On the other hand, seamless steel tubes are produced without any welding process. They offer superior durability and resistance to pressure, making them suitable for critical automotive components such as fuel lines, hydraulic systems, and exhaust systems.</p></p>
