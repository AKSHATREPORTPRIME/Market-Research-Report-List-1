Water-soluble film-forming agents dissolve easily in water and are commonly used in products like moisturizers and foundations. On the other hand, oil-soluble film-forming agents dissolve in oil and are often found in products like lipsticks and mascaras. These agents provide various benefits such as increased longevity, improved spreadability, and enhanced adherence of cosmetics.</p></p>
