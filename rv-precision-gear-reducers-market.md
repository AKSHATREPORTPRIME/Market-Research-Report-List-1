<p><p>RV Precision Gear Reducers are categorized into two main types - Flange Output Type and Hollow Type. Flange Output Type refers to gear reducers that have a flange output interface, allowing for direct mounting of output components. This makes them suitable for applications that require precision alignment or connection to other devices. On the other hand, Hollow Type gear reducers have a hollow structure, allowing the input or output shaft to pass through the center. This makes them suitable for applications that require space-saving designs or integration with other mechanical components.</p></p>
