A key driver for this growth is the increasing consumer awareness regarding the importance of a balanced diet and the need for essential minerals like zinc. Thus, the rising demand for zinc-fortified food products is driving the growth of the zinc phytate market.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising health consciousness among consumers and the growing trend of vegetarian and vegan diets are also contributing to the market growth. As zinc phytate is naturally occurring, it aligns with the growing demand for clean label and chemical-free ingredients in various industries, including food and cosmetics.</p><p>Overall, with the increasing consumer awareness regarding the importance of zinc and the rising demand for fortified food products, the zinc phytate market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period.</p></p>
