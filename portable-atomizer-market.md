<p><p>The portable atomizer market comprises three main types: Pneumatic Atomizer, Ultrasonic Atomizer, and Net Atomizer. Pneumatic Atomizers use compressed air or gas to break down liquid into small droplets. Ultrasonic Atomizers use high-frequency vibrations to create a fine mist. Net Atomizers utilize a fine mesh or net to break down liquid into tiny particles. All three types are designed for portable use, offering convenience and versatility for personal and professional applications where delivering liquids in atomized form is required.</p></p>
