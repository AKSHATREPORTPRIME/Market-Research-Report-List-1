<p><p>The dust-free paper market comprises various types such as latex-bonded airlaid paper, thermal-bonded airlaid paper, and multi-bonded airlaid paper. Latex-bonded airlaid paper is created by combining natural latex and airlaid fibers, resulting in a high-strength material suitable for food packaging and medical applications. Thermal-bonded airlaid paper is made by heating and bonding airlaid fibers using thermoplastic resins, providing excellent absorbency and softness, commonly used in wipes and hygiene products. Multi-bonded airlaid paper involves multiple layers of airlaid fibers bonded together, offering enhanced strength and absorbency for industrial and personal care products.</p></p>
