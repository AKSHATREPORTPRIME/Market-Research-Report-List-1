These pipes offer excellent resistance against corrosive chemicals, making them a preferred choice in such industries.</p><p>Secondly, the growing water treatment industry is also fueling the demand for PTFE lined pipes. These pipes are used in water treatment plants to transport corrosive chemicals and acids safely. With the increasing focus on water quality and environmental regulations, the demand for PTFE lined pipes is expected to rise.</p><p>Additionally, the expanding food and beverage industry is contributing to the market's growth. As the food and beverage industry continues to expand, the demand for PTFE lined pipes is projected to increase.</p><p>In conclusion, the PTFE lined pipes market has a positive outlook with expected growth in the coming years.
