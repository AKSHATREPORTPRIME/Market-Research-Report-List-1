This helps identify variations and mutations in the entire genome. Whole exome sequence analysis focuses on analyzing only the protein-coding regions of the genome, which are believed to be responsible for most genetic diseases. Other market types refer to additional services such as transcriptome analysis (studying gene expression patterns) or epigenomic analysis (examining chemical modifications of DNA). These different market types cater to distinct research or diagnostic needs in genomics.</p></p>
