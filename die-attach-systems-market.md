<p><p>Die attach systems are used in the semiconductor industry to bond the microchips or dies onto substrates or lead frames. Fully automatic die attach systems are designed to complete the entire process of die bonding without any human intervention. They are highly efficient and capable of handling a large number of dies in a short time. Semi-automatic systems require some human interaction during the process, such as manual loading and unloading of the dies. Other types of die attach systems may include manual or customized solutions tailored to specific requirements.</p></p>
