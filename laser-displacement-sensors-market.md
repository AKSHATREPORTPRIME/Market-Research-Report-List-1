Digital laser sensors use a digital output signal to provide precise measurements and are suitable for applications that require high accuracy. On the other hand, CMOS laser sensors use a complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor (CMOS) image sensor and offer advantages such as compact size, low power consumption, and high speed. Both types of sensors are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, robotics, and automation for various applications.</p></p>
