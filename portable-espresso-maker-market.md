<p><p>Portable Espresso Maker market is categorized into three types: Manual, Semi-Automatic, and Automatic. Manual espresso makers are operated by hand, requiring the user to manually control the pressure and timing of the extraction process. Semi-Automatic machines, on the other hand, automate some aspects of the process, such as temperature control and water flow, but still require the user to initiate the extraction and control the grind size. Automatic espresso makers are fully automated, with built-in timers and sensors, allowing for a simplified and convenient brewing experience without requiring user intervention.</p></p>
