<p><p>Industrial non-mobile equipment hydraulic cylinders can be categorized into two types: rod and telescopic. Rod cylinders are designed with a single rod that extends and retracts to provide linear force in one direction. They are commonly used in applications that require pushing or pulling tasks. On the other hand, telescopic cylinders consist of several nested sleeves that extend and retract, allowing for a greater stroke length. This type is often preferred in applications where a longer reach is needed or space is limited, such as construction machinery or dump trucks.</p></p>
