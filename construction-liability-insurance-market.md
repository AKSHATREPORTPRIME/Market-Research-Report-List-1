D&O Insurance, also known as Directors and Officers Insurance, protects company directors and officers from legal actions against them individually. It covers claims related to mismanagement, negligence, or breach of duty. E&O Insurance, or Errors and Omissions Insurance, safeguards professionals or businesses offering services against claims of negligence, mistakes, or inadequate work. Both policies ensure financial protection from lawsuits and liabilities, but D&O Insurance focuses on executives and E&O Insurance caters to professionals offering services.</p></p>
