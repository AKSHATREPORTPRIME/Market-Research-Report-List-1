<p><p>Medical-grade silicone rubber is a highly versatile material used in various medical applications. There are two main types: High Consistency Rubber (HCR) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HCR has a solid, rubber-like consistency and is typically used in applications such as medical tubing and seals. LSR, on the other hand, is a liquid material that can be molded into complex shapes and is commonly used for medical implants and devices. Both HCR and LSR offer excellent biocompatibility, durability, and resistance to extreme temperatures.</p></p>
