<p><p>The flexible PV cell market consists of three types: CIGS, a-Si, and OPV. CIGS (copper indium gallium selenide) technology offers high conversion efficiency and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. a-Si (amorphous silicon) cells are economical and offer good low-light performance but have relatively lower efficiency. OPV (organic photovoltaics) cells are lightweight and cost-effective, although they have lower efficiency than other types. Each of these market types caters to different needs and preferences, allowing for a diverse range of flexible solar cell options in the market.</p></p>
