<p><p>The automotive structural sheet metal market can be divided into two main types: chassis and exterior. Chassis sheet metal components are used to form the frame and support structure of a vehicle, providing strength and rigidity. These components include floor pans, body mounts, crossmembers, and reinforcements. On the other hand, exterior sheet metal components are used to create the outer body of the vehicle, including doors, hoods, fenders, and roofs. These components contribute to the aesthetics and aerodynamics of the vehicle. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the safety, functionality, and overall appearance of automobiles.</p></p>
