<p><strong>The Sealing Products in Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FFKM</li><li>FKM</li><li>VMQ</li><li>EPDM</li><li>PTFE</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealing products in the semiconductor market can be categorized into various types. FFKM (perfluoroelastomers) are highly resistant to extreme temperatures and chemicals, ensuring a tight seal. FKM (fluoroelastomers) possess excellent resistance to solvents and temperature extremes. VMQ (silicone rubber) provides good electrical insulation and high flexibility. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) offers excellent weather resistance, making it suitable for outdoor applications. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its low friction and high-temperature resistance. Other types may include NBR (nitrile rubber) or HNBR (hydrogenated nitrile rubber), depending on the specific sealing requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>Sealing products play a crucial role in various semiconductor market applications such as dry/wet etching, plasma systems, chemical vapor deposition (CVD), atomic layer deposition (ALD), physical vapor deposition (PVD), and others. In dry/wet etching, sealing products ensure precise and controlled removal of materials during the etching process. In plasma systems, they create a tight seal to prevent leakage of plasma gases. In CVD, ALD, and PVD, sealing products maintain a vacuum-tight environment for accurate deposition of thin films. Altogether, these sealing products guarantee the efficiency, accuracy, and reliability of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
