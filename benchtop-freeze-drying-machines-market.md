<p><p>Benchtop freeze drying machines, also known as laboratory freeze drying machines, are compact-sized equipment used for the removal of moisture from various materials through a freeze-drying process. Similarly, the pharmaceutical industry relies on benchtop freeze drying machines for drug development, vaccine production, and storage of valuable biological samples.</p><p>Moreover, the current COVID-19 pandemic has further boosted the demand for benchtop freeze drying machines.
