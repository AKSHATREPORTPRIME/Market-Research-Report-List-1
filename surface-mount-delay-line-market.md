<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The surface mount delay line market consists of two main types: passive and active. Passive delay lines are characterized by their simple structure and lack of active components. They rely on passive circuit elements such as capacitors and resistors to introduce the desired delay in the signal. On the other hand, active delay lines include active components such as transistors or integrated circuits that amplify or regenerate the signal while introducing the required delay. Each type of delay line has its own advantages and applications, depending on the specific requirements of the electronic system.</p></p>
