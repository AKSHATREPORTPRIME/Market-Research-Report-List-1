<p><p>The non-network connected non-IC card power smart meter market consists of two types: simplex and three-phase meters. Simplex meters are typically used for single-phase electrical connections and provide basic energy consumption measurements. They do not have networking capabilities, meaning they cannot transmit data wirelessly. On the other hand, three-phase meters are used for powering commercial or industrial establishments and can measure the energy consumption of three-phase electrical connections. Like simplex meters, they lack networking features and cannot be connected to a network for data transmission.</p></p>
