<p><ul><li>Visible Watermark</li><li>Invisible Watermark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Watermark packaging service market types can be categorized into two: visible watermark and invisible watermark. Visible watermark refers to the printing of a logo, text, or design on the packaging material, making it easily visible to the naked eye. This type of watermark is often used for branding and promotional purposes. On the other hand, invisible watermark involves embedding a unique code or pattern onto the packaging, which can only be detected using special technology or under specific conditions, offering a more discreet authentication and anti-counterfeiting solution.</p></p>
