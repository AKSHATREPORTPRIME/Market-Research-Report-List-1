<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Industry Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Twin Screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical industry extruder market is comprised of two types: single screw and twin screw. Single screw extruders consist of a rotating screw that moves the material through the barrel, applying heat as necessary. This type is commonly used for low-viscosity materials and offers simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Twin screw extruders, on the other hand, have two intermeshing screws that provide better mixing capabilities and are suitable for a wider range of materials. They offer enhanced process control and flexibility, making them preferred for more complex pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
