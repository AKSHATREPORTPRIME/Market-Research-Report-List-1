<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Brake System market is comprised of three main types: Traction Control System (TCS), Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), and Electronic Stability Control (ESC). TCS helps prevent the wheels from losing traction during acceleration. ABS prevents the wheels from locking up during braking, enhancing control and stability. ESC, on the other hand, helps maintain vehicle stability by detecting and reducing skidding or loss of control. These systems form a crucial part of automotive safety, improving driving performance and reducing the risk of accidents.</p></p>
