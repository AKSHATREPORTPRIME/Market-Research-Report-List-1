Quasi-Continuous Wave Fiber Lasers emit pulses of light at a high repetition rate, resulting in a quasi-continuous beam. Other types include mode-locked fiber lasers and pulsed fiber lasers, which generate short and intense pulses of light. These different types cater to diverse applications and industries, offering flexibility and versatility in laser usage.</p></p>
