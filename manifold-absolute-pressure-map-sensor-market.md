This allows for continuous monitoring and provides real-time information about the engine's performance. Analog sensors are commonly used in older vehicles.</p><p>On the other hand, the Digital Sensor market utilizes MAP sensors that convert the intake manifold pressure into digital signals. These sensors offer higher accuracy, faster response times, and better compatibility with modern engine control systems. They are commonly found in newer vehicles.</p></p>
