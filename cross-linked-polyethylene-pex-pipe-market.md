<p><p>Cross-linked polyethylene (PEX) pipe is a flexible plastic tubing commonly used in plumbing systems. PEX-A, PEX-B, and PEX-C are different types of cross-linked polyethylene pipe distinguished by their manufacturing processes. PEX-A is produced by the Peroxide method, resulting in the most flexible and durable pipe with superior resistance to cracks. PEX-B is made by the Silane method, offering a balance between flexibility and cost-effectiveness. PEX-C is manufactured using the Electron Beam method, resulting in a moderately flexible and affordable pipe option. These different types provide a range of options to suit various plumbing needs.</p></p>
