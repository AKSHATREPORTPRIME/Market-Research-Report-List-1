<p><p>The low-floor tram market consists of two types: the 100% low-floor tram and the 70% low-floor tram. A 100% low-floor tram refers to a tram where the entire vehicle is at the same level as the platform, allowing easy access for passengers with mobility challenges. On the other hand, a 70% low-floor tram has a majority of its passenger area at platform level, with a smaller section equipped with steps for entry. These market types cater to different accessibility needs and preferences in public transportation.</p></p>
