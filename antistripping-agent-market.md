<p><p>Antistripping agents are used in asphalt pavements to prevent moisture damage, specifically the separation of asphalt binder from aggregate particles. The market for antistripping agents can be categorized into three types: amine anti-stripping agents, lime anti-stripping agents, and other alternatives. Amine anti-stripping agents are chemical additives that are effective in reducing moisture susceptibility. Lime anti-stripping agents, on the other hand, utilize hydrated lime to enhance the adhesion between asphalt binder and aggregate. Other alternatives refer to different types of materials or techniques that are used to mitigate the risk of stripping in asphalt pavements.</p></p>
