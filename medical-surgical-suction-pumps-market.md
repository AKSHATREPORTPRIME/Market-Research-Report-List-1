Electric suction pumps are powered by electricity and are efficient for continuous use, often used in hospitals and clinics. Manual suction pumps are hand-operated and provide portable and cost-effective options for emergency scenarios or remote areas without power supply. Pneumatic suction pumps use air pressure to create suction and are commonly found in operating rooms for surgical procedures. These three types cater to different needs and provide options for various medical situations.</p></p>
