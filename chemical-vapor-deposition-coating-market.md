<p><p>Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) coating is a method used for depositing thin, high-performance coatings on various surfaces such as metals, ceramics, and polymers. In this process, a solid material is evaporated into a vapor phase and then thermally decomposed or chemically react with a reactive gas, resulting in a thin film formation on the substrate. CVD coating offers several advantages such as high adhesion, uniform thickness, and excellent chemical resistance.</p><p>The global Chemical Vapor Deposition Coating market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period.
