<p><strong>The Car Wrap Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Film</li><li>Calendered Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car wrap film market comprises two main types: cast film and calendered film. Cast film is known for its superior quality and durability. It is produced by pouring the liquid material onto a casting sheet and allowing it to solidify. This process creates a thin, flexible film that adheres well to curves and contours, providing a smooth and seamless finish. Calendered film, on the other hand, is made by pressing the material between rollers. While less expensive, it is generally thicker and stiffer, making installation more challenging and potentially resulting in a less polished appearance.</p></p>
