<p><p>Automotive fuel additives market consists of two major types: gasoline additives and diesel additives. Gasoline additives are specifically designed to improve the performance and efficiency of gasoline-powered vehicles. They help to clean fuel injectors, reduce engine deposits, reduce emissions, and enhance overall engine performance. Diesel additives, on the other hand, are formulated to enhance the performance and longevity of diesel engines. They provide lubrication to reduce friction, prevent corrosion, improve fuel economy, and decrease emissions. Both types of additives aim to optimize the functionality and environmental friendliness of automotive fuel.</p></p>
