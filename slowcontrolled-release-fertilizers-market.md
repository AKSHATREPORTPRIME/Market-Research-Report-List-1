<p><p>Slow/Controlled Release Fertilizers are special types of fertilizers that release the nutrients at a slow and controlled rate, providing a continuous supply of nutrients to plants over an extended period. Slow/controlled release fertilizers help reduce nutrient leaching and volatilization, which can have adverse effects on soil and water quality. One notable trend is the increasing adoption of polymer-coated fertilizers, which provide a uniform and controlled nutrient release.
