<p><p>Plastic rapid prototyping machine market consists of different types such as rapid prototyping, 3D printing, and rapid casting. Rapid casting, on the other hand, employs molds to replicate complex shapes in a fast and efficient manner. These machines play a crucial role in various industries by enabling speedy, accurate, and cost-efficient production of plastic prototypes and parts.</p></p>
