<p><p>Decorative High Pressure Laminates (HPL) can be classified into two types based on their application markets: horizontal market and vertical market. In contrast, the vertical market refers to the application of HPL on vertical surfaces like walls, cabinets, and doors. In this context, the HPL provides enhanced structural strength, resistance to scratches and impacts, and a variety of decorative options to enhance interior design.</p></p>
