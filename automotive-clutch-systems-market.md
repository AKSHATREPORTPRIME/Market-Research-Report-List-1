<p><ul><li>Manual Transmission</li><li>Automatic Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive clutch systems can be categorized into two main market types: manual transmission and automatic transmission. In a manual transmission, the driver manually engages and disengages the clutch using a clutch pedal. This type provides more control over gear shifting but requires more effort from the driver. On the other hand, automatic transmission systems use a fluid coupling or torque converter to automatically engage and disengage the clutch, eliminating the need for manual control. This offers a smoother driving experience but may limit the driver's control over gear shifting.</p></p>
