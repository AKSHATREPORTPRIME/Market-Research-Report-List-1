<p><p>Medical/diagnostic imaging includes various types of technologies used for diagnosing diseases and conditions. X-ray machines capture images of the body's internal structures using low-dose radiation. Computed Tomography (CT) scans create detailed cross-sectional images through X-ray and computer processing. Ultrasound equipment uses sound waves to create images of organs and tissues. MRI equipment uses a powerful magnetic field and radio waves to generate detailed images of the body. Nuclear imaging involves the use of radioactive materials to create images that show how organs and tissues function, aiding in diagnosis.</p></p>
