<p><p>Glass printing ink is a specialized type of ink used for printing on glass surfaces. There are two main types of glass printing ink: oily glass ink and water glass ink. Oily glass ink is oil-based and provides a durable and long-lasting print on glass. It is resistant to water and chemicals. On the other hand, water glass ink is water-based and offers a more environmentally friendly solution. It dries quickly and is suitable for printing on smooth glass surfaces. Both types have their own advantages and applications in the glass printing industry.</p></p>
