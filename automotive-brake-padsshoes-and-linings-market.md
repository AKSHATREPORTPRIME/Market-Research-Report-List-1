<p><p>The automotive brake pads, shoes, and linings market consists of three key product types used in vehicle braking systems. Brake pads are flat, metallic or ceramic plates that grip the brake rotor to slow down or stop the vehicle. Brake shoes are curved metal parts that press against the inside of a brake drum to generate friction. Brake linings are made of high-friction materials and are used to line the surface of brake shoes, enhancing their stopping power. These products are critical for ensuring efficient and safe braking performance in automobiles.</p></p>
