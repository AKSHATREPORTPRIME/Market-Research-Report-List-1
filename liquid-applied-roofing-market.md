<p><ul><li>Bituminous Membranes</li><li>Elastomeric Membranes</li><li>Cementitious Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid applied roofing market includes different types such as bituminous membranes, elastomeric membranes, and cementitious membranes. Bituminous membranes are made from a blend of bitumen and a carrier sheet, providing excellent waterproofing and durability. Elastomeric membranes are made from synthetic rubber and offer high flexibility and resistance to weathering. Cementitious membranes consist of cement, fillers, and polymers, ensuring high strength and crack resistance. These liquid applied roofing options provide various benefits such as seamless installation, easy repairs, and long-lasting protection against water infiltration.</p></p>
