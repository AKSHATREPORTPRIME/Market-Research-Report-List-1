<p><p>The industrial lubricants market comprises three main types: conventional, synthetic, and bio-based/re-refined. Conventional lubricants are traditional petroleum-based products, widely used due to their versatile nature. Synthetic lubricants, on the other hand, are chemically engineered and provide superior performance properties like stability, resistance, and enhanced lubrication. Bio-based/re-refined lubricants are derived from renewable sources or recycled petroleum products, offering environmental benefits. Each type caters to specific needs and industries, with synthetic lubricants gaining popularity due to their advanced properties, and bio-based/re-refined lubricants being a sustainable option.</p></p>
