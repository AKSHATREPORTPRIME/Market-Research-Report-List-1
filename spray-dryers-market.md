<p><p>Spray dryers are a crucial equipment in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. The pressure spray dryer applies pressure to atomize the liquid, ensuring a fine mist before drying it. Stream spray dryers utilize high-velocity streams to atomize the liquid, leading to faster drying. Centrifuging spray dryers employ centrifugal force to atomize the liquid, facilitating drying. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications, providing industries with efficient and effective drying solutions to meet their specific requirements.</p></p>
