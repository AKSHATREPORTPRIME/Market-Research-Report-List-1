<p><p>The adhesive films market is segmented into various types, including pressure sensitive adhesive films, hot-melt adhesive films, light-cured adhesive films, and others. Pressure sensitive adhesive films are designed to stick when light pressure is applied and can be easily removed without leaving residue. Hot-melt adhesive films are applied as a molten material and solidify upon cooling. Light-cured adhesive films are cured by exposure to ultraviolet light. Other types of adhesive films include solvent-based and water-based films, each offering unique properties and applications in industries such as packaging, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
