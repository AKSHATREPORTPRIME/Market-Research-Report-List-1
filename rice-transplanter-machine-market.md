<p><p>The rice transplanter machine market can be classified into two types: riding type and walking type. </p><p>Riding type machines are larger and are operated by a person sitting on top of the machine. These machines are more suitable for large-scale rice cultivation as they have higher capacity and can cover more land in a shorter time.</p><p>Walking type machines, on the other hand, are smaller and are operated by a person walking behind the machine. These machines are more commonly used in small-scale rice farms where maneuverability and flexibility are important.</p><p>Both types of machines aim to efficiently transplant rice seedlings into the field, but the choice depends on the scale and requirements of the rice cultivation operation.</p></p>
