<p><p>The hem flange joint adhesive market can be categorized into two types: one component and two component. One component adhesives are pre-mixed, ready-to-use adhesives that do not require any additional mixing or preparation. They are simple to apply and provide quick and easy bonding solutions. On the other hand, two component adhesives consist of two separate ingredients that need to be mixed together before application. These adhesives offer greater flexibility in terms of curing time and bond strength, making them suitable for a wider range of applications.</p></p>
