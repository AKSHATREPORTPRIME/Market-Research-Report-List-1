<p><p>The Magnesium Casting market encompasses two major types, namely Magnesium die-casting and Magnesium sand-casting. Magnesium die-casting involves the process of injecting molten magnesium into a mold cavity under high pressure, allowing for the production of intricately shaped parts with excellent dimensional accuracy. On the other hand, Magnesium sand-casting utilizes molds made of sand to cast the molten magnesium, resulting in less precise parts but offering more flexibility in terms of design and cost. Both methods play a crucial role in meeting the diverse needs of the magnesium casting market.</p></p>
