<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Solenoids are essential components in internal combustion engines that control the timing and duration of valve openings for better performance and fuel efficiency. The market for VVT solenoids comprises two types: Normally Closed and Normally Opened. Normally Closed VVT solenoids stay closed when not in use and open when activated, allowing oil pressure to control the valve timing. Conversely, Normally Opened VVT solenoids stay open in the resting state and close when activated, regulating the flow of oil pressure to adjust valve timing.</p></p>
