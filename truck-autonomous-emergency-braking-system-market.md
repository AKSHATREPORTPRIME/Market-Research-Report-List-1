<p><p>The Truck Autonomous Emergency Braking System market consists of three types: Forward Emergency Braking, Reverse Emergency Braking, and Multi-Directional Emergency Braking. Forward Emergency Braking is designed to detect potential collisions in front of the truck and automatically apply the brakes to prevent or mitigate the impact. Reverse Emergency Braking operates similarly but focuses on detecting obstacles when the truck is in reverse gear. Multi-Directional Emergency Braking provides all-around collision prevention by detecting objects from multiple angles, offering enhanced safety during complex maneuvers.</p></p>
