<p><p>The vehicle exhaust purification system market primarily consists of two types: three-way catalyst and oxidation catalyst. A three-way catalyst helps in reducing emissions of harmful gases such as nitrogen oxides (NOx), carbon monoxide (CO), and hydrocarbons (HC), by converting them into less harmful gas species. On the other hand, an oxidation catalyst predominantly focuses on converting carbon monoxide and unburned hydrocarbons into carbon dioxide and water. Both types of catalysts contribute to reducing vehicle emissions, thereby promoting cleaner air and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
