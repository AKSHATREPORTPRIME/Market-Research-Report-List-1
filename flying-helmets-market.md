<p><p>The flying helmets market primarily consists of three types: open face type, integral type, and others. Open face type helmets have a design that covers the head but leaves the face exposed. They are suitable for pilots who require clear communication and easy access to their face. Integral type helmets, on the other hand, fully cover the head and offer more protection. They are preferred for their safety features and noise reduction properties. Finally, the "others" category includes specialized flying helmets catering to specific needs such as night vision or microlight aviation.</p></p>
