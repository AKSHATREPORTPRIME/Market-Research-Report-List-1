<p><p>The electric vehicle battery pack market is applied in various types of vehicles, including hybrid electric vehicles (HEV), plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEV), fully electric vehicles (EV), and fuel cell vehicles (FCV). HEVs use a combination of an internal combustion engine and an electric motor, with the battery pack providing power to the motor during certain driving conditions. EVs solely rely on the battery pack for electric propulsion, offering long-range driving without emitting any tailpipe emissions. FCVs use hydrogen and a fuel cell stack to generate electricity that powers the vehicle, with the battery pack often utilized as a buffer to store and release energy efficiently.</p></p>
