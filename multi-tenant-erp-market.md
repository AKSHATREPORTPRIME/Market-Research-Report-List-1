<p><p>Multi-tenant ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) refers to a software architecture in which multiple organizations or tenants can share a single instance of the software application. Multi-tenant ERP solutions are predominantly cloud-based, allowing organizations to access their data and applications from anywhere, at any time, with improved agility and flexibility.</p><p>Additionally, the need for streamlining business processes and enhancing operational efficiency is fueling the demand for multi-tenant ERP solutions.
