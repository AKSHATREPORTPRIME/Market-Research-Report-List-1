<p><p>Polyurethane sealants are widely used in the marine market for various applications. There are two main types of polyurethane sealants available: single-component and multi-component. Single-component sealants are pre-mixed and ready to use, requiring no additional mixing agents. They are easy to apply and cure when exposed to moisture. On the other hand, multi-component sealants come in separate parts that need to be mixed before application. They offer advanced bonding capabilities and are typically used for demanding marine applications that require superior strength and durability.</p></p>
