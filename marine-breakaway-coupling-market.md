<p><p>Marine Breakaway Couplings (MBCs) are essential safety devices that prevent spillage and damage during offshore oil and gas transfers. Petal Valve MBCs use a series of overlapping petals to seal the flow, while Flip-Flap MBCs utilize spring-loaded flaps. Both types of MBCs function by automatically disconnecting the fluid transfer system when excessive tension or pressure occurs, thus preventing a full-scale rupture or spill. These market types cater to different customer requirements and environmental conditions, offering flexibility and reliability in maintaining safe operations in marine industries.</p></p>
