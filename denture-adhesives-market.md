<p><strong>The Denture Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Restoration Bonding</li><li>Indirect Restoration Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Denture adhesives are used to enhance the stability and comfort of dentures. The direct restoration bonding type involves the adhesive being directly applied to the inner surface of the denture and bonded directly to the gums. This type is more common for temporary dentures. On the other hand, indirect restoration bonding involves the adhesive being applied to the abutment, which is then attached to the implant or natural tooth. This type is typically used for permanent dentures and provides a stronger and more long-lasting bond.</p></p>
