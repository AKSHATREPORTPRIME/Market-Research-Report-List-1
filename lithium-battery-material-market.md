<p><p>The lithium battery material market comprises two main types: cathode material and anode material. Cathode material is responsible for storing and releasing lithium ions during charge and discharge processes. It is usually made from various materials such as lithium cobalt oxide, lithium iron phosphate, and lithium nickel cobalt aluminum oxide. On the other hand, anode material acts as the host for lithium ions. It is commonly composed of graphite or silicon-based materials. Both cathode and anode materials play crucial roles in the efficiency and performance of lithium batteries.</p></p>
