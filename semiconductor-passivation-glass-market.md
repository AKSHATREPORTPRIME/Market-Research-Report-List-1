<p><ul><li>Wafer Passivation</li><li>Diode Encapsulation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor passivation glass is widely used in various applications, including wafer passivation and diode encapsulation. Wafer passivation is the process of applying a protective layer on the surface of semiconductor wafers to prevent contamination and enhance reliability. This helps in improving the performance and lifespan of the semiconductor devices. On the other hand, diode encapsulation involves using passivation glass to protect diode devices, ensuring their long-term reliability and durability. Both wafer passivation and diode encapsulation are essential in the semiconductor industry to maintain and enhance the performance of electronic components.</p></p>
