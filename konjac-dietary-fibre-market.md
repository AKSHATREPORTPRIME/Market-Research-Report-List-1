On the other hand, purified konjac dietary fibre undergoes additional processing steps, such as washing and filtration, to remove impurities and enhance its quality. The purified market offers a higher degree of purity and may be preferred for applications where a higher quality and cleaner product is desired, such as in the food industry.</p></p>
