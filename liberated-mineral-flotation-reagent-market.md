<p><p>The Liberated Mineral Flotation Reagent Market is categorized into four types: Flotation Collectors, Flotation Depressants, Flotation Activators, and Others. Flotation Collectors are chemicals that help in the collection of valuable minerals during flotation. Flotation Depressants are substances used to inhibit the flotation of certain unwanted minerals. Flotation Activators are chemicals that enhance the flotation process by increasing mineral recovery. The category of Others includes reagents that do not fall under the previous types, such as pH modifiers and frothers. These reagents play crucial roles in the liberation of minerals during the flotation process.</p></p>
