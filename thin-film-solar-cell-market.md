<p><strong>The Thin-film Solar Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CdTe Thin-film Solar Cells</li><li>CIS/CIGS Thin-film Solar Cells</li><li>a-Si Thin-film Solar Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thin-film solar cell market consists of various types, including CdTe thin-film solar cells, CIS/CIGS thin-film solar cells, and a-Si thin-film solar cells. CdTe thin-film solar cells are made from cadmium telluride, while CIS/CIGS thin-film solar cells use copper indium selenide or copper indium gallium selenide. On the other hand, a-Si thin-film solar cells are composed of hydrogenated amorphous silicon. These types of thin-film solar cells offer advantages such as flexibility, lightweight, and low production costs, making them increasingly popular in the solar energy market.</p></p>
