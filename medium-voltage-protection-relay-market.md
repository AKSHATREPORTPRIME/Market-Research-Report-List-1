<p><strong>The Medium-voltage Protection Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical & Static Relay</li><li>Digital & Numerical Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium-voltage protection relay market consists of two main types: electromechanical relay and static relay. Electromechanical relays use mechanical components like springs and magnetic coils to detect abnormalities and interrupt the electricity flow. Static relays, on the other hand, rely on semiconductor materials for detection and interruption. Another categorization is the digital and numerical relay market, where digital relays process signals in a non-numeric form, while numerical relays convert signals into numeric data for analysis and decision-making. These different types offer varying levels of accuracy, speed, and functionality in protecting medium-voltage electrical systems.</p></p>
