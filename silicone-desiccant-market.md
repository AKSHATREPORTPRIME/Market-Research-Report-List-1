<p><p>The silicone desiccant market comprises three main types: white desiccant, blue desiccant, and orange desiccant. White desiccant is made from silica gel and is widely used due to its non-toxic and odorless properties. Blue desiccant, also made from silica gel, contains a cobalt chloride indicator that changes color when saturated. It is commonly used in industries where humidity control is crucial. Orange desiccant is made from a blend of activated clay and calcium sulfate. It is non-toxic and eco-friendly, often used in applications such as food packaging to preserve freshness.</p></p>
