<p><p>The Professional Broadcast LCD Monitor market is categorized into two types: Active LCD Monitors and Passive LCD Monitors. Active LCD Monitors utilize an LED backlight system to project images with enhanced brightness and contrast, producing vibrant and realistic visuals. On the other hand, Passive LCD Monitors employ a thin-film transistor technology that reduces energy consumption and provides wider viewing angles. Both types cater to the professional broadcast industry, offering high-quality display solutions for applications such as video editing, color grading, and broadcasting.</p></p>
