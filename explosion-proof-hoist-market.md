<p><p>The explosion-proof hoist market includes two main types: chain hoists and wire rope hoists. Chain hoists are designed with a lifting mechanism that consists of a metal chain and a hook, enabling them to lift heavy loads vertically. Wire rope hoists, on the other hand, utilize a system of wire ropes wound around a drum, providing strength and flexibility in lifting operations. Both types of hoists are specifically designed to operate safely in hazardous environments where the risk of explosions is present.</p></p>
