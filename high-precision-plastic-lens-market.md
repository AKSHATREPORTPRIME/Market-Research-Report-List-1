<p><p>The high precision plastic lens market encompasses two main types: aspheric lenses and spherical lenses. Aspheric lenses have a more complex surface shape, enabling them to correct aberrations and provide better image quality compared to spherical lenses. They are widely used in fields such as cameras, eyeglasses, and medical devices. On the other hand, spherical lenses have a simpler curved surface, making them suitable for applications that do not require high precision, such as basic magnification or focusing tasks. Both types serve different purposes in the market, catering to the needs of various industries.</p></p>
