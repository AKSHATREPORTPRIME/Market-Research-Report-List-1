<p><p>The vacuum tanker market consists of three main types: liquid suctioning only, liquid and dry suctioning, and high velocity. Liquid suctioning only tankers are designed solely for collecting and transporting liquid substances. Tankers with liquid and dry suctioning capabilities are capable of collecting both liquid and solid materials. On the other hand, high velocity tankers are specifically designed to handle materials such as sand, stones, or debris with high speed and force. These different types cater to specific needs and requirements in various industries, including waste management and cleaning services.</p></p>
