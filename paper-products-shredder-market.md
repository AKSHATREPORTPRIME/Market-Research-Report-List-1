<p><p>The paper products shredder market is divided into three main types: strip cut, cross cut, and micro cut shredders. Strip cut shredders cut documents into long, narrow strips, providing basic security for disposing of personal information. Cross cut shredders, on the other hand, produce smaller confetti-like pieces, offering a higher level of security. Micro cut shredders offer the highest level of security by shredding documents into tiny particles. These different types cater to various security needs, with strip cut being the most basic, followed by cross cut, and micro cut providing the highest level of security.</p></p>
