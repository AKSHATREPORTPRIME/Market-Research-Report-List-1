<p><p>The Silicon Wafer Auto Clean Equipment market comprises two types of cleaning methods: Wet Clean and Dry Clean technologies. Wet Clean involves the use of liquid solutions or chemicals to remove contaminants and impurities from silicon wafers. It is commonly utilized for removing organic residues. On the other hand, Dry Cleaning uses gaseous substances such as plasma or vapor to clean the wafers without any liquid involvement. This method is often favored for removing inorganic particles, such as oxide residues. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the cleanliness and quality of silicon wafers used in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
