<p><p>Waterborne UV curable resins are water-based resins that cure when exposed to ultraviolet (UV) light. These resins are widely used in various industries, including coatings, inks, adhesives, and electronics. They offer fast curing, excellent adhesion, and enhanced chemical and abrasion resistance, making them an ideal choice for a wide range of applications.</p><p>The global waterborne UV curable resins market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. By resin type, acrylics, polyurethanes, and epoxy are the major segments. Acrylic-based resins are the most widely used as they provide excellent adhesion and resistance properties.
