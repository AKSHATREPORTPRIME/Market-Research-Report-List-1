<p><p>The electromagnetic (EMI) shielding market includes two main types: polymer EMI shielding materials and metal EMI shielding materials. Polymer EMI shielding materials are made of conductive polymers that are lightweight and flexible, providing good electrical conductivity and shielding effectiveness. They are commonly used in electronic devices and communication systems. Metal EMI shielding materials, on the other hand, are made of metals like copper, aluminum, and stainless steel, providing high shielding effectiveness. They are used in applications where higher shielding performance is required, such as military equipment and medical devices.</p></p>
