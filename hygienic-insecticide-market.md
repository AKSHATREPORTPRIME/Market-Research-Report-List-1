<p><p>The hygienic insecticide market encompasses various types of insecticide products. Powder insecticides are finely ground substances that can be applied directly to surfaces or mixed with water to create a spray. Smoke agents release insecticides when ignited, creating a fog that targets insects. Aerosol insecticides are pressurized cans that disperse fine mists of insecticide when sprayed. Emulsifier insecticides are oil-based liquids that can be mixed with water to create an emulsion for spraying. Finally, there are other types of insecticides available, such as granules or baits, each designed for specific applications and pest control needs.</p></p>
