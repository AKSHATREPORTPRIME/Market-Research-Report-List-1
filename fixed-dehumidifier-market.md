<p><p>The fixed dehumidifier market is classified into different types based on their functioning. Refrigerant dehumidifiers use a refrigeration system to cool the air and condense moisture, making them energy-efficient for large areas. Desiccant dehumidifiers utilize a desiccant material to absorb humidity from the air, making them suitable for cold environments. Ventilated dehumidifiers use ventilation systems to circulate and remove humid air from enclosed spaces. Other types include hybrid dehumidifiers and whole-house dehumidifiers, which offer additional features and applications. These various types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers in the fixed dehumidifier market.</p></p>
