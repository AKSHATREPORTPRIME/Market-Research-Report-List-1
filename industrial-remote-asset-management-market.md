<p><p>Industrial Remote Asset Management involves the management and monitoring of assets remotely. The market can be classified into two types - On-premises and Cloud-based. On-premises asset management refers to systems where the software and infrastructure are installed and operated locally by the company. On-premises systems typically provide higher security and control, while cloud-based systems offer more flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
