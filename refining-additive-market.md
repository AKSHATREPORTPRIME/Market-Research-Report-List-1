<p><p>The refining additive market encompasses various types of additives used in the refining process. Corrosion inhibitors are chemicals that prevent or minimize the corrosion of metals in the refining equipment. Antioxidants inhibit the oxidation process of hydrocarbons, ensuring their stability. Defoamers eliminate foam formation during refining operations. Biocides control the growth of microorganisms in the refining process to maintain product quality. "Others" refers to additional types of refining additives, such as demulsifiers, dispersants, and scale inhibitors, which serve specific purposes in the refining industry.</p></p>
