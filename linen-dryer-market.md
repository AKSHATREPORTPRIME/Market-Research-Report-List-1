<p><p>The linen dryer market consists of various types of dryers, including fluidized bed dryers, conduction dryers, convection dryers, and rotary dryers. Fluidized bed dryers use hot air to suspend and dry linen particles, while conduction dryers transfer heat directly to the linen through contact with heated surfaces. Convection dryers circulate heated air around the linen, promoting rapid drying. Rotary dryers utilize rotating drum-like structures to dry linen using heated air. Each type of dryer offers unique advantages and is suitable for specific linen drying applications.</p></p>
