<p><p>Polypropylene foams are a type of thermoplastic foam that are widely used in various industries due to their lightweight and cost-effective properties. There are two main types of polypropylene foams: expanded polypropylene (EPP) foams and extruded polypropylene (XPP) foams. EPP foams are created by expanding small polypropylene beads using heat and pressure, resulting in a highly porous and durable foam. XPP foams, on the other hand, are made by extruding molten polypropylene through a die, creating a continuous and uniform foam structure. Both EPP and XPP foams offer excellent insulation, cushioning, and impact absorbing capabilities, making them ideal for packaging, automotive, and construction applications.</p></p>
