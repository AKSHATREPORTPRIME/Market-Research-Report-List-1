<p><ul><li>Air Suspension System</li><li>Integrated Suspension System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive performance suspension system market is divided into two types: air suspension system and integrated suspension system. </p><p>Air suspension systems use air-filled bags or bellows that support the vehicle's weight and provide a smooth ride by adjusting the air pressure. These systems can be adjusted for different road conditions and vehicle loads, improving comfort and handling.</p><p>Integrated suspension systems combine various suspension components, such as shock absorbers, springs, and control arms, into a single unit. This integrated design improves overall suspension performance, offering better handling, stability, and comfort for the driver and passengers.</p></p>
