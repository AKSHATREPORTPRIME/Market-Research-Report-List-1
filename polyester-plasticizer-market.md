<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Polyester Plasticizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester Plasticizers are chemical compounds added to polyester resins to increase their flexibility and reduce brittleness. Type I polyester plasticizers are low molecular weight compounds that offer excellent plasticizing performance but are sensitive to heat and can migrate to the surface of the material over time. On the other hand, Type II polyester plasticizers are high molecular weight compounds that are more stable and have better resistance to heat. They provide superior flexibility and durability, making them suitable for applications that require high-performance plasticized polyester materials.</p></p>
