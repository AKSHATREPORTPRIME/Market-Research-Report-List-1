<p><strong>The Automotive Infrared Night Vision System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Infrared Night Vision System</li><li>Passive Infrared Night Vision System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Infrared Night Vision System market consists of two main types: Active Infrared Night Vision System and Passive Infrared Night Vision System. The Active Infrared Night Vision System uses infrared sensors and illuminators to detect objects in low-light or pitch-dark conditions. It emits infrared light beams that bounce back off objects and are detected by the sensors, allowing the driver to see clearly. On the other hand, the Passive Infrared Night Vision System uses thermal imaging cameras to detect the heat emitted by objects, converting it into an image visible to the driver.</p></p>
