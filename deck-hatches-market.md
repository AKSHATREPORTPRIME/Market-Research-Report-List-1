<p><p>Deck Hatches are classified into two market types based on their functionality: Watertight and Non-Watertight. Watertight deck hatches are designed to prevent water ingress, ensuring a sealed and secure environment. They are commonly used in marine vessels, offshore platforms, and other water-related applications. On the other hand, Non-Watertight deck hatches do not provide a water seal, making them unsuitable for areas that require water resistance. They can be found in various sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial buildings, where water protection may not be a primary concern.</p></p>
