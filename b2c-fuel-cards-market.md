<p><p>The B2C fuel cards market can be categorized into two types: active cards and non-active cards. Active cards refer to fuel cards that are still being actively used by customers for purchasing fuel. They are actively swiped or scanned at fuel stations, and the corresponding amount is deducted from the card's balance. Non-active cards, on the other hand, are fuel cards that are no longer in use or have a zero balance. These cards may have expired, been canceled, or are simply not being utilized by the customer anymore.</p></p>
