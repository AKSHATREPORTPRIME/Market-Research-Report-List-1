<p><p>Textured Soy Protein (TSP), also known as textured vegetable protein or soy meat, is a plant-based protein derived from soybeans. The rise in vegan and vegetarian populations, combined with the increasing number of people reducing their meat consumption, is boosting the demand for meat substitutes like TSP.
