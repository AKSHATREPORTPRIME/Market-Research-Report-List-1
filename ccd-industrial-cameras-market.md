<p><p>The CCD Industrial Cameras Market consists of two main types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. Area scan cameras capture an entire image at once, suitable for inspecting stationary objects or capturing images within a certain area. On the other hand, line scan cameras capture images line by line, ideal for inspecting moving objects or capturing images of long objects. Both types play crucial roles in industrial applications such as quality control, sorting, robotics, and manufacturing by providing high-quality imaging capabilities.</p></p>
