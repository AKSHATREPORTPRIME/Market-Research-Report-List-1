<p><p>Baby thermometers are used to measure a baby's body temperature accurately. There are two main types of baby thermometers in the market. Electronic thermometers provide quick and precise temperature readings by using electric sensors. They are often used orally or axillary (under the arm). Infrared thermometers, on the other hand, measure temperature by detecting infrared radiation emitted by the baby's body. They are non-contact, making them ideal for use on fussy or sleeping babies. Both types of thermometers are widely available and serve the same purpose of monitoring a baby's temperature.</p></p>
