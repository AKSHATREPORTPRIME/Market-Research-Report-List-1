<p><p>A low level infeed palletizer is a type of industrial equipment used in the packaging industry to automatically stack products, such as boxes or bags, onto pallets. This equipment operates at a lower level compared to high level palletizers, making it a suitable choice for facilities with limited ceiling space. Low level infeed palletizers typically feature a conveyor system that transports products to a stacking area, where robotic or mechanical arms arrange them into layers on a pallet. The stacked pallet is then typically moved out of the system for shipping or storage.</p><p>The low level infeed palletizer market is expected to exhibit significant growth in the coming years. The increasing demand for automation in the packaging industry to improve efficiency and reduce labor costs is a major driving factor for the market.
