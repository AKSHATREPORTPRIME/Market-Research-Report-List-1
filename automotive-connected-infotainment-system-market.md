<p><p>The Automotive Connected Infotainment System market consists of different types of systems such as Embedded Systems, Smartphone Integrated Systems, and Tethered Systems. Smartphone Integrated Systems allow users to connect their smartphones to the vehicle's infotainment system, accessing various apps and features. Tethered Systems involve connecting the vehicle to external devices, like smartphones or tablets, via cables or wireless connections, enabling access to specific infotainment functions. These different types cater to diverse consumer preferences and provide various levels of connectivity and convenience.</p></p>
