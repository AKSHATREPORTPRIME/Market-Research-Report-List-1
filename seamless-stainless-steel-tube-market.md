<p><p>The seamless stainless steel tube market is categorized into two types: cold finished tubes and hot finished tubes. Cold finished tubes are produced through a process that involves cold drawing and annealing, resulting in a smooth and precise finish. These tubes are commonly used in applications that require high dimensional accuracy and excellent surface quality. On the other hand, hot finished tubes are created through a process that involves hot extrusion or piercing, which provides a rougher surface finish. These tubes are typically used in applications where dimensional tolerances are less critical, such as structural or mechanical purposes.</p></p>
