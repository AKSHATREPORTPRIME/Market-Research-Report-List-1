<p><p>Commercial sous vide machines are categorized into immersion types and water bath types. Immersion types are compact and portable devices that can be directly placed into a water container, allowing the food to be cooked evenly using heated circulating water. Water bath types, on the other hand, consist of a large vessel filled with water that is heated with built-in elements. The food is placed in sealed pouches and submerged in the water, ensuring precise temperature control and even cooking. Both types cater to the needs of the commercial market, providing professional chefs and foodservice establishments with efficient and consistent cooking methods.</p></p>
