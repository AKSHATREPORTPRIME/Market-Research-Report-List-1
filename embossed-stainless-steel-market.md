<p><p>The embossed stainless steel market is categorized into three types: the 200 series, 300 series, and 400 series. The 200 series consists of stainless steel that contains nickel as the main alloying element and has low corrosion resistance. The 300 series comprises stainless steel with both nickel and chromium, offering good corrosion resistance and durability. The 400 series consists of stainless steel with high carbon content, making it less corrosion resistant but more affordable. These variations provide customers with different options based on their specific requirements for design, budget, and longevity.</p></p>
