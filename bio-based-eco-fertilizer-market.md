<p><p>The bio-based (eco) fertilizer market comprises different types, including nitrogen-fixing, phosphate-solubilizing, potash-mobilizing, and others. Nitrogen-fixing bio-fertilizers contain microorganisms that convert atmospheric nitrogen into a usable form for plants, promoting healthier growth. Phosphate-solubilizing bio-fertilizers release bound phosphorus in the soil, enhancing its availability to plants. Potash-mobilizing bio-fertilizers aid in the absorption of potassium from the soil, supporting plant development and stress tolerance. Other types may include bio-fertilizers that provide additional nutrients, improve soil structure, or offer pest and disease control. These variants contribute to a sustainable and environmentally friendly approach to agriculture.</p></p>
