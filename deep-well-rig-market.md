<p><p>The deep well rig market can be classified into three types: rotary drilling rig, impact rig, and compound drilling rig. Rotary drilling rigs work by rotating a drill bit to create a hole in the ground. Impact rigs use a percussive action to break rocks, while compound drilling rigs combine the functions of both rotary and impact rigs. These different types of rigs cater to various geologies and drilling requirements, allowing for efficient and effective deep well drilling operations.</p></p>
