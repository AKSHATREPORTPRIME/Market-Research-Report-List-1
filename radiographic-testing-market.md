<p><p>The Radiographic Testing market includes various types of technologies used for non-destructive testing. Film Radiography involves capturing images on photographic film. Real Time Radiography (RTR) allows for real-time imaging using electronic detectors. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed cross-sectional images using X-rays. Digital Radiography (DR) uses digital sensors to produce high-quality images instantly. Computed Radiography (CR) utilizes a phosphor imaging plate for capturing X-ray images that can be digitally processed. These technologies play a crucial role in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and healthcare for flaw detection and quality control purposes.</p></p>
