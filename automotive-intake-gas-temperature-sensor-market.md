<p><p>The Automotive Intake Gas Temperature Sensor market is segmented into various types. Thermistor type sensors measure temperature by the change in resistance of a ceramic element. Thermocouple type sensors use the principle of voltage generation at the junction of two different metals to determine temperature. Semiconductor-based type sensors utilize a semiconductor element whose resistance changes with temperature. Other types of sensors include mass airflow sensors, which measure the quantity of air entering the engine, and infrared sensors, which use infrared radiation to determine temperature.</p></p>
