<p><p>Tire flaps are rubber components used in tires to protect them from damage and extend their lifespan. There are two main types of tire flaps: radial tire flaps and bias tire flaps. Radial tire flaps are designed for radial tires, which have ply cords running radially from the bead to the tread. Bias tire flaps, on the other hand, are meant for bias tires that have ply cords crossing diagonally. Each type of tire flap is specifically engineered to suit the construction and performance characteristics of its corresponding tire type.</p></p>
