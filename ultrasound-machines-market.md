<p><p>Ultrasound machines are medical devices used for imaging internal body structures. They come in different types: A-Mode measures depth and amplitude of echoes, B-Mode or 2D Mode provides a 2D image of the scanned area, C-Mode allows continuous scanning in a single plane, M-Mode displays movement over time, Doppler Mode measures blood flow velocity, Pulse Inversion Mode improves the detection of lesions, and Harmonic Mode reduces noise and enhances image quality. These modes cater to diverse medical requirements and aid in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
