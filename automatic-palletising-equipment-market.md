<p><p>The automatic palletising equipment market consists of two main types: mechanical type and robotic type. The mechanical type involves using mechanical arms or grippers to handle and stack pallets. These systems are typically less expensive and suitable for less complex palletising tasks. On the other hand, the robotic type employs robots equipped with advanced sensors and programming capabilities to handle, sort, and stack pallets autonomously. While more expensive, these systems offer greater flexibility, speed, and precision, making them the preferred choice for complex and high-speed palletising operations.</p></p>
