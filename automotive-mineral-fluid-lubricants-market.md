<p><p>The automotive mineral fluid lubricants market includes three main types of lubricants: paraffinic, naphtenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from paraffin bases and offer excellent oxidation stability. Naphtenic lubricants are formulated from naphthenic bases and provide high solvency and low pour points, making them suitable for extreme temperatures. Aromatic lubricants are made from aromatic bases and have good chemical stability and resistance to oxidation. These lubricants play a crucial role in maintaining the performance and longevity of automotive machinery and engines.</p></p>
