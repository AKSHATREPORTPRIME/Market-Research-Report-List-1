<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Coatings</li><li>Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic coatings market types typically include solvent-based coatings and powder coating. Solvent-based coatings consist of organic solvents that are evaporated during the curing process, resulting in a solid protective layer on the plastic surface. These coatings offer excellent adhesion, durability, and resistance to chemicals and abrasion. On the other hand, powder coating involves the application of dry powder onto the plastic substrate, which is then heated to fuse and form a resilient coating. Powder coatings are known for their environmental friendliness, durability, and versatility in creating different finishes and textures.</p></p>
