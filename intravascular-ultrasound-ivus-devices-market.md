<p><p>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) devices are medical imaging devices that use sound waves to create detailed images of the blood vessels from inside the body. It involves the insertion of a catheter with an ultrasound probe into the blood vessels, allowing for the visualization of the vessel walls, plaques, and blood flow. IVUS devices are commonly used to diagnose and treat cardiovascular diseases, such as atherosclerosis, by providing real-time imaging guidance during procedures like stenting.</p><p>The global Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Devices Market is poised for significant growth, with a projected Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 4.7% during the forecast period. The growing demand for minimally invasive procedures and the advancements in IVUS technologies are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing a shift towards the adoption of integrated systems that combine IVUS imaging with other imaging modalities, such as Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) and angiography. These integrated systems provide comprehensive imaging solutions, enabling better diagnosis and treatment planning.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a growing focus on expanding applications of IVUS devices beyond coronary artery disease.
