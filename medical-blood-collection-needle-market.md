<p><p>The medical blood collection needle market can be categorized into two types: with holder and without holder. A blood collection needle with holder includes a plastic handle or holder that provides a firm grip during the blood collection process. This type of needle is usually preferred for conventional venipunctures. On the other hand, a blood collection needle without holder does not have a plastic handle and is directly inserted into the vein by the healthcare professional. This type of needle is used for more advanced or specific procedures, such as arterial blood sampling or power injections.</p></p>
