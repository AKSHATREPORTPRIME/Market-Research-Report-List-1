<p><p>Sterilizer steam generators are used in various industries to produce steam for sterilization purposes. There are two types of these generators: integral boiler and remote boiler. Integral boiler generators are designed with a built-in steam boiler, providing a compact and self-contained unit. On the other hand, remote boiler generators require a separate boiler to produce steam, which is then piped to the sterilizer unit. Both types have their advantages depending on the specific requirements of the sterilization process, including space availability and demand for steam production.</p></p>
