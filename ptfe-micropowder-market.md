<p><strong>The PTFE Micropowder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monomer Polymerization</li><li>Resin Degradation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE micropowder market can be classified into two types: monomer polymerization and resin degradation. Monomer polymerization refers to the process where PTFE is synthesized from its monomer form through a chemical reaction. In this market, PTFE micropowder is used as an additive in various industries such as lubricants, paints, and coatings. On the other hand, the resin degradation market involves the breakdown of PTFE resins into micropowder form. These micropowders are then utilized in applications like friction materials, textiles, and electrical insulation.</p></p>
