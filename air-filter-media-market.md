<p><p>The air filter media market is categorized into three types: HEPA Grade Media, MERV Grade Media, and ULPA Grade Media. HEPA grade media is designed to capture particles as small as 0.3 microns, making it highly efficient for removing allergens and pollutants. MERV grade media offers various filtration levels, suitable for different applications, including residential, commercial, and industrial. ULPA grade media is a high-performance filter that can capture ultrafine particles down to 0.1 microns, making it ideal for cleanrooms and other sensitive environments. These media types cater to diverse air filtration needs across industries.</p></p>
