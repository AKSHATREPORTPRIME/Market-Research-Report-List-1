<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The OLED-on-Silicon Microdisplays market consists of three types: Self-Luminous Type, Transmissive Type, and Reflective Type. The Self-Luminous Type microdisplays emit their own light, requiring no additional backlighting, resulting in better contrast and color reproduction. Transmissive Type microdisplays allow light to pass through, necessitating a backlight for illumination. Reflective Type microdisplays reflect external light, eliminating the need for a backlight and offering wider viewing angles. Each type caters to different needs, offering distinct advantages and applications within the market.</p></p>
