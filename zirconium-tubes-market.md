Zr 704 is an alloy with higher tensile strength compared to Zr 702, making it useful in industries like chemical processing. Zr 705 is an alloy that combines the high strength of Zr 704 with enhanced corrosion resistance, offering optimal performance in aggressive environments such as nuclear reactors and chemical plants.</p></p>
