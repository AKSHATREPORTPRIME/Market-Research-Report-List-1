<p><strong>The Automotive Crankshaft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forged Steel</li><li>Cast Iron/Steel</li><li>Machined Billet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive crankshaft market can be categorized into three main types: forged steel, cast iron/steel, and machined billet. Forged steel crankshafts are robust and durable, offering high strength and resistance to fatigue. Cast iron/steel crankshafts are cost-effective and commonly used in mass-produced vehicles. Machined billet crankshafts are precision-engineered from a solid piece of metal, providing superior performance but at a higher cost. Each type has its advantages and is used in different applications based on factors such as vehicle type, budget, and desired performance level.</p></p>
