<p><strong>The Acoustic Vapor Leak Detection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Onboard Sensors</li><li>Structural Load Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acoustic Vapor Leak Detection System Market includes two main types: Onboard Sensors and Structural Load Sensors. Onboard Sensors are designed to detect vapor leaks in real-time directly from the source, such as storage tanks or pipelines. These sensors are typically installed internally within the vessel and utilize advanced acoustic technology for accurate and reliable detection. On the other hand, Structural Load Sensors are installed externally on the structure where the vapor is stored. They measure and monitor the load changes caused by vapor leaks, providing an indirect indication of leaks in the system.</p></p>
