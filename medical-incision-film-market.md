<p><p>Medical incision film is used to cover surgical wounds and prevent infection. It comes in various types, including PU film, PE film, and iodophor film. PU film, made of polyurethane, is flexible, breathable, and hypoallergenic, providing a comfortable healing environment. PE film, or polyethylene film, is cost-effective and offers good moisture resistance, protecting the wound during the healing process. Iodophor film contains iodine, which has antimicrobial properties, further reducing the risk of infection. These different film types cater to different needs and preferences in the medical field.</p></p>
