<p><p>Air flow switches are devices used to detect the air velocity in HVAC systems or industrial applications. Type I air flow switches are designed to measure low air speeds, typically in the range of 10-300 feet per minute (fpm). They are commonly used in residential HVAC systems or cleanrooms. On the other hand, Type II air flow switches are suitable for higher air velocities, typically ranging from 300-10,000 fpm. They are often found in commercial or industrial applications, such as ventilation systems in factories or warehouses.</p></p>
