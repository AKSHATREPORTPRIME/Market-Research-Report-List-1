<p><strong>The Aircraft Thrust Reverser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cascade Type</li><li>Pivot Four Door</li><li>Bucket Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are three types of aircraft thrust reversers in the market. Cascade type reversers redirect the exhaust gases from the engine through a series of small doors or vanes, which slows down the aircraft. Pivot four-door reversers have four panels that pivot outward to redirect the exhaust gases and reduce the aircraft's forward thrust. Bucket type reversers have large doors that swing open to create a bucket-like shape, forcing the exhaust gases forward, thus providing reverse thrust to slow down the aircraft.</p></p>
