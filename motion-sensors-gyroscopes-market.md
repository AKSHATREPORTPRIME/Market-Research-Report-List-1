<p><strong>The Motion Sensors Gyroscopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrating Gyroscope</li><li>Rate Gyroscope</li><li>Unconstrained Gyro</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motion Sensors Gyroscopes can be classified into three main types: Integrating Gyroscope, Rate Gyroscope, and Unconstrained Gyro. An Integrating Gyroscope measures the angle of rotation by integrating the angular velocity over time. A Rate Gyroscope provides continuous angular velocity measurement without integration. On the other hand, an Unconstrained Gyro doesn't have any physical limitations on its range of motion, allowing it to measure rotations in any direction. These different types offer varying advantages and are used in different applications, such as navigation systems, gaming consoles, and drones.</p></p>
