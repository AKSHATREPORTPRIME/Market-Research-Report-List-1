<p><p>The warehouse forklift market is divided into two major types: electric forklifts and internal combustion forklifts. Electric forklifts are powered by batteries and provide a quiet and environmentally friendly option suitable for indoor use. They are generally smaller and have a lower weight capacity. On the other hand, internal combustion forklifts run on gasoline, diesel, or propane engines, making them ideal for outdoor or heavy-duty applications. They offer higher lifting capacities and faster speeds but generate noise and emissions.</p></p>
