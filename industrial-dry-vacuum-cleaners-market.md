<p><p>Industrial dry vacuum cleaners are categorized into two types: electric and pneumatic. Electric industrial dry vacuum cleaners are powered by electricity and are widely used in industries for their effectiveness in cleaning large areas. They are equipped with high-performance motors, filters, and collection tanks. On the other hand, pneumatic industrial dry vacuum cleaners use compressed air to create suction, making them suitable for hazardous environments where electricity is restricted. They are commonly used in industries like oil and gas, chemical, and mining, offering safe and efficient cleaning solutions.</p></p>
