<p><p>The Spent Fuel Pool Level Instrumentation System market offers different types of systems to measure the level of spent fuel pools. Guided Wave Radar System uses a probe to measure the level by sending microwave signals and analyzing the reflected signals. Through-Air Radar System operates similarly but without the need for a probe, using radio waves instead. Bubbler System measures the level by comparing the pressure of air bubbles to the water pressure in the pool. Lastly, there are other systems available in the market that utilize different technologies for level measurement.</p></p>
