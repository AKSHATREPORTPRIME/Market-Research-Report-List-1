<p><p>The TiAl alloy market consists of two main types: Gamma Type and Other Type. Gamma Type alloys have a high aluminum content and are primarily used in high-temperature applications due to their superior oxidation resistance. These alloys are widely adopted in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and power generation. The Other Type alloys, on the other hand, have lower aluminum content and offer better ductility and fatigue resistance. They find applications in areas like chemical processing, marine, and biomedical sectors. Both types contribute to the diverse and evolving TiAl alloy market.</p></p>
