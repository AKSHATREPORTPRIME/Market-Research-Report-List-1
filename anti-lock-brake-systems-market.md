One-channel ABS operates on one wheel, usually found in older vehicles. Two-channel ABS works on two wheels independently, commonly seen in motorcycles. Three-channel ABS operates on three wheels, usually found in trucks or SUVs. Four-channel ABS is the most advanced and operates on all four wheels independently, offering the highest level of control and effectiveness, commonly found in modern cars.</p></p>
