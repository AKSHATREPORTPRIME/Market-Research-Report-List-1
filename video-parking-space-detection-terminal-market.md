<p><p>A Video Parking Space Detection Terminal is a device that uses video analytics and computer vision technology to accurately detect and monitor the occupancy of parking spaces in real-time. With the increasing problem of parking congestion in urban areas, the demand for efficient parking management systems has been growing rapidly. These factors are driving the adoption of video parking space detection terminals by both public and private organizations.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies are further enhancing the capabilities of video parking space detection terminals.
