<p><p>Metalworking cutting fluids are used in industrial processes to cool and lubricate metal surfaces during cutting operations. There are four types of cutting fluids: neat oil, emulsion, semi-synthetic, and synthesis. Neat oil fluids are pure oils and provide excellent lubrication but may require frequent maintenance. Emulsion fluids are oil-water mixtures and offer good cooling and lubricating properties. Semi-synthetic fluids combine oil with water and additives for efficient cooling and lubrication. Synthetic fluids are fully formulated and offer superior performance in terms of cooling, lubrication, and rust protection.</p></p>
