<p><p>Ground detector relays are used in electrical systems to sense and detect ground faults. There are two main types of ground detector relays: AC market and DC market. </p><p>AC ground detector relays are specifically designed for alternating current electrical systems. They can quickly identify ground faults in AC systems and trip the circuit interrupter to protect the system from further damage. </p><p>On the other hand, DC ground detector relays are designed for direct current electrical systems. They function similarly to AC relays but are specifically tailored to detect ground faults in DC systems. Their purpose is to ensure the safety and reliability of the electrical system by quickly identifying and reacting to any ground faults.</p></p>
