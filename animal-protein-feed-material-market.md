<p><p>The animal protein feed material market encompasses various types of feed materials derived from animals. Fish meal is made from processing fish, containing high levels of protein, minerals, and vitamins. Blood meal is created from dried animal blood, rich in protein and iron. Plasma protein meal is obtained from animal blood plasma, with high protein content. Feather meal is made from chicken feathers, providing a good source of protein. Meat and bone meal is produced by rendering animal tissues, containing protein and minerals. Leather meal is derived from waste leather, providing a protein source. Insect protein feed refers to the use of insects as a protein-rich feed material.</p></p>
