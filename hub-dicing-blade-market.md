Manufacturers are developing innovative hub dicing blades to cater to the specific requirements of different materials, including silicon, glass, ceramics, and compound semiconductors.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a growing focus on the development of ultra-thin hub dicing blades to address the challenges of cutting thinner and smaller chips.
