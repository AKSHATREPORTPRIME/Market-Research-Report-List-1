<p><p>Dry running rotary vane vacuum pumps are widely used in various industries for vacuum applications. These pumps operate without the need for lubricating oil, reducing maintenance and environmental concerns. There are two main types of dry running rotary vane vacuum pumps: single-stage and two-stage. </p><p>A single-stage vacuum pump has a simple design with a single rotor and stator arrangement. It is suitable for low vacuum applications. In contrast, a two-stage vacuum pump consists of two sets of rotors and stators, allowing it to achieve a higher vacuum level. It is commonly used in applications that require a deeper vacuum. Both types play a crucial role in meeting diverse vacuum requirements in industries.</p></p>
