<p><ul><li>Clip-On Type</li><li>Adhesive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire balance weight market consists of two main types: clip-on type and adhesive type. Clip-on type refers to weights that are attached to the wheel by clipping onto the rim. These weights are often made of metal and can be easily installed and removed when necessary. On the other hand, adhesive type weights are applied to the wheel by using a strong adhesive. These weights are usually made of lead or zinc and provide a more permanent solution for balancing tires.</p></p>
