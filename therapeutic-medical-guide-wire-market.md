<p><p>The Therapeutic Medical Guide Wire Market can be categorized into two types: Solid Guide Wire and Wrapped Guide Wire. A Solid Guide Wire is a single-piece wire made of stainless steel, while a Wrapped Guide Wire consists of a core wire that is coated with a polymer material. Solid Guide Wires are known for their high flexibility, torque control, and reduced friction, making them suitable for complex procedures. Wrapped Guide Wires, on the other hand, offer improved visibility due to the polymer coating and are commonly used in applications such as angioplasty and stenting.</p></p>
