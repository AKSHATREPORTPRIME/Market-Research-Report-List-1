<p><strong>The GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal</li><li>Optical Network Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) equipment market includes two main types: Optical Line Terminal (OLT) and Optical Network Terminal (ONT). An Optical Line Terminal is a device in the service provider's central office responsible for managing and controlling the overall GPON network. It aggregates and forwards data traffic to subscriber Optical Network Terminals. On the other hand, an Optical Network Terminal is located at the subscriber's premises and serves as the interface between the subscriber's devices and the GPON network, providing high-speed internet, voice, and video services.</p></p>
