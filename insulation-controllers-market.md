<p><p>Insulation controllers are devices used in electrical power systems to support and insulate power lines to prevent electrical leakage. There are three main types: suspension, line post, and braced line post. Suspension insulation controllers are used to suspend power lines at predetermined heights and distances, ensuring safe operation. Line post insulation controllers are installed on power poles to provide insulation and support for the lines. Braced line post insulation controllers are similar to line post controllers but have additional bracing for extra stability and support. These controllers play a crucial role in maintaining safe and reliable electrical power transmission.</p></p>
