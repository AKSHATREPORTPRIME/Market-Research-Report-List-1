<p><p>The UAM Electri Vertical Takeoff and Landing Aircrafts market includes two main types: Vector Propulsion and Vectorless Propulsion. In Vector Propulsion, the aircraft is propelled by multiple thrusters, which can be tilted in different directions to enable vertical takeoff and landing. On the other hand, Vectorless Propulsion relies on a single propulsor located centrally, which uses thrust vectoring technology to achieve the same vertical maneuvers. These two types offer different propulsion mechanisms for UAM aircrafts, catering to diverse operational requirements and design preferences in the market.</p></p>
