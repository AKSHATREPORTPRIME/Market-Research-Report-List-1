<p><p>Laser welding diamond saw blades are used for cutting hard materials like concrete and stone. There are two main types of markets for these blades: dry operation and wet operation. In dry operation, the blades are used without any water or coolant, which makes them suitable for smaller jobs and reduces the risk of water damage.
