<p><p>The Sapphire Materials and Components market can be classified into two main types: Skarn Type and Hydrothermal Type. Skarn Type refers to sapphires that are formed in metamorphic rocks, particularly in limestone or dolomite environments. These sapphires are typically rich in iron and aluminum. On the other hand, Hydrothermal Type sapphires are formed in fissures within igneous or metamorphic rocks through the action of hot fluids. These sapphires usually have a high clarity and are often used in electronics, optics, and jewelry manufacturing.</p></p>
